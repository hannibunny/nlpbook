Radar trends to watch: December 2020Trends in AI, Robotics, Infrastructure, and more. This month’s collection of interesting articles that point to important trends is dominated by AI. That’s not surprising; AI has probably been the biggest single category all year. But its dominance over other topics seems to be increasing.
