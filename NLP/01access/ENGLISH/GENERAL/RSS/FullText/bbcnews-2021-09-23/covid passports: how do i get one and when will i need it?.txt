Covid passports: How do I get one and when will I need it?New Covid passport rules will begin in Scotland and Wales in October, restricting who can enter a nightclub or attend large-scale events.Similar plans in England have been scrapped, but will be kept in reserve in case they are needed this winter. What are the Covid passport plans for Scotland?From 1 October, people will need a Covid vaccination certificate to enter nightclubs, adult entertainment venues and large-scale events - although the Night Time Industries Association Scotland, representing nightclubs, is launching a legal challenge against the proposals.The Scottish government says a nightclub is anywhere that meets all of the following criteria:  You can only get the passport if you have been fully vaccinated in Scotland. Proof of a recent negative test will not be enough.Some people will be exempt, including under-18s, people taking part in vaccine trials and those unable to be vaccinated for medical reasons.People can prove they're fully vaccinated by using the NHS Scotland Covid Status App (available from 30 September) to generate a QR code. A paper version is also available.The Scottish government says it's working to ensure the system will also work for people vaccinated in the rest of the UK, or abroad. What are the new rules for Wales?From 11 October, people will be able to use the NHS Covid Pass to show they have been vaccinated.It will also be available to people who have had a negative lateral flow test in the past 48 hours. What happened to Covid passport plans in England?The government decided not to go ahead with vaccine passports, despite saying they would be introduced at the end of September.Setting out the government's winter Covid plans, Health Secretary Sajid Javid said the idea might be reconsidered, if cases surge.The plans had faced opposition from some Tory backbenchers and the Liberal Democrats. Nightclubs said they were "chaotic and surreal". Call 0300 303 5667 for a paper copy at least five days after your final dose.Currently, anyone 16 or over can go online and download a PDF version of their vaccination status showing a unique QR code.
